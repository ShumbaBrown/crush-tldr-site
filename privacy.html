Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Crush-TLDR ("we", "our", or "the App") is operated by Yard & Y'all Technologies. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address (or phone number), display name, home city, and preferred airport code(s). If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email from those providers.

Travel preferences: Preferred travel days, trip duration, cabin class, price limits, and stop preferences you set in the app.

Partner pairing: When you pair with a partner, we store partner IDs and a couple identifier to enable shared features like trip planning and countdowns.

Trip and activity data: Planned trips, saved flight watchlists, trip itineraries, and activity recommendations you save or complete.

Device and diagnostic data: When you submit feedback, we collect device type, operating system version, app version, current screen, and a log of recent in-app events (navigation and API calls) to help us diagnose issues. We do not collect this data at any other time.

Push notification tokens: If you enable notifications, we store your device push token to send price alerts and trip reminders.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect or store passwords (authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication).
  • We do not collect location data or GPS coordinates.
  • We do not read your contacts, photos, or messages.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize flight search results and visit recommendations.
  • To enable partner pairing and shared trip planning features.
  • To send push notifications for price drops and trip reminders (if enabled).
  • To diagnose and fix bugs when you submit feedback.
  • To improve the app based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, and cloud functions. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Amadeus — Flight search data. We send airport codes and travel dates; no personal information is shared. See Amadeus Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini — Activity recommendations. We send destination city names only; no personal information is shared.
  • Expo — Push notification delivery. See Expo Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore, hosted in the United States. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to your data is protected by Firebase Security Rules that enforce per-user ownership.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account through the app, all associated data (profile, trips, watchlists, preferences, and feedback) is permanently deleted from our systems.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and update your data at any time in the Settings screen.
  • Delete your account and all associated data from Settings.
  • Disable notifications at any time in your device settings or in the app.
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

Crush-TLDR is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
